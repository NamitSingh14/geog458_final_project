About the project

Through this project, we will attempt to visualize minimum and maximum temperatures across the counties in the United States, along with the precipitation rate. Since the idea is to showcase climate change, the project will have the temperature and precipitation readings from different years. This project would be intended for the general public, specifically those who have an interest in the chosen location (the U.S). This could also benefit climate change activists and other people trying to study climate change in any location in the USA.

Project Impact

Understanding climate change and the patterns in temperature/precipitation changes can help us keep the ecosystem in a healthy condition. If we are ignorant about climate change, it can cause natural disasters, and human health issues, and damage both man-made and natural systems. This project should make it easier for people to visualize climate change and take appropriate action.

Primary Functions

  • Hover on counties on the map to get a detailed information
  • Year slider from 2000 to 2015 to let the user select a specific time period
  • Month slider to view specific months for a given year
